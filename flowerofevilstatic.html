Flower of Evil: A Review

Date: January 2022
Rating: 9.8 out of 10

Synopsis

"Flower of Evil," a suspense melodrama staring Lee Joong Gi , Moon Chae Won and Kim Ji Hoon, began airing July 29th 2020. Lee Joon Gi takes on the role of Baek Hee Seung, who is reality is someone else entirely. He hides his identity from his detective wife Cha Ji Won, protrayed by Moon Chae Woon. Kim Ji Hoon portrays the real Baek Hee Sung.

The Acting

With a line-up of talented leads to supporting roles, the drama is set up to not only engage viewers but also convey a slew of deeply moving scenes.

The Plot

In addition to it's amazing cast, the drama also comes to life thanks to it's exceptional plot. Like a true suspense drama, it keeps viewers guessing till the very end.

The Editing

Some dramas fall victim to sub par editing, but not "Flower of Evil." It continues to deliver beyond the cast and screenplay into the technical aspects.
